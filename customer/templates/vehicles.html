OUR VEHICLES

Explore our range of eco-friendly vehicles, designed to meet the needs of urban transport. Whether you're looking for a quick ride or a longer journey, our e-scooters and e-bikes offer the perfect solution to navigate the city efficiently while reducing your carbon footprint.

E-Scooters

Our e-scooters are built for speed and convenience. Perfect for short city trips, they are eco-friendly and easy to use. With an intuitive control system and a top speed of 20mph, you'll navigate through urban environments with ease. They are lightweight, portable, and can be quickly charged, making them ideal for busy commuters. Safety is a priority, and each e-scooter comes equipped with advanced braking systems and headlights for night rides.

  • Top speed: 20mph
  • Age requirement: 16+
  • Price: £0.30 per minute
  • Weight capacity: 220 lbs (100 kg)

E-Bikes

Our e-bikes combine the traditional bicycle experience with electric-powered assistance. Perfect for those who need a little help on long rides or hilly terrains, our e-bikes allow you to pedal with ease. Pedal-assist technology adjusts automatically to match your pace, making uphill climbs effortless. With adjustable gears, ergonomic seating, and extended battery life, our e-bikes are suitable for both commuting and recreational rides. They are also equipped with built-in GPS, so you can track your rides in real-time.

  • Top speed: 30mph
  • Age requirement: 14+
  • Price: £0.40 per minute
  • Pedal-assist modes: Eco, Standard, Power
