GUIDELINES

Safety Guidelines

Safety is our priority. Follow these guidelines to ensure a safe and enjoyable ride:

  • Always wear a helmet while riding.
  • Be mindful of pedestrians and other vehicles on the road.
  • Check the brakes and tires before starting your ride.
  • Only use e-scooters if you are at least 16 years old, and e-bikes if you are at least 14.
  • Obey all traffic rules and signs.

Parking Guidelines

To keep our cities clean and accessible, follow these parking guidelines:

  • Park your vehicle in designated parking areas marked for e-scooters and e-bikes.
  • Do not block sidewalks, entryways, or pedestrian crossings when parking.
  • Always park your vehicle upright and lock it securely.
  • Avoid parking in restricted zones, like private properties or in front of driveways.

Riding Zones

Be aware of the designated riding zones for e-scooters and e-bikes:

  • Stick to bike lanes or shared-use paths when available.
  • Avoid riding on sidewalks unless permitted in your area.
  • Use caution when riding in areas with heavy traffic.
  • Check the app for restricted or no-ride zones in your city.

Other Important Guidelines

  • Respect your fellow riders and pedestrians.
  • Report any damaged or malfunctioning vehicles through the app.
  • Follow local laws and regulations for riding e-scooters and e-bikes.
